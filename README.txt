== Description ==

Would you like to put surveys anywhere on your web pages? This survey plugin gives you an easy way to do that. And why would you want surveys on your web pages? Here are some reasons:

• Let your visitors rate your services, your business.

• See if your readers find your articles helpful.

• Get reactions to your web changes before implementing them.

• Quiz your visitors on their preferences

• Conduct branding surveys on any topic you want!

SurveyFunnel Plus plugin offers a unique solution to get your visitors' feedback directly on your WordPress website.

**Important Links:**

* [More Details](http://app.surveyfunnel.com/)

== Installation ==

1. Upload `SurveyFunnel Plus` folder to the `/wp-content/plugins/` directory
2. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress
3. Register to apps.surveyfunnel.com
4. Enter & Validate your App key
5. Visit your Apps Account to manage your Surveys
